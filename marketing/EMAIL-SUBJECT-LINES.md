# EMAIL SUBJECT LINE VARIATIONS
A/B test these for best open rates

---

## URGENCY-BASED

1. `URGENT: [domain] accessibility score 0/100`
2. `[domain]: 102 critical violations detected`
3. `⚠️ [domain] accessibility alert`
4. `Your site scored [X]/100 - needs attention`
5. `[domain] ADA compliance warning`

---

## CURIOSITY-BASED

1. `I scanned [domain] - you should see this`
2. `Quick question about [domain]'s accessibility`
3. `[domain] - found something interesting`
4. `Ran your site through our scanner...`
5. `[Company] accessibility results`

---

## VALUE-BASED

1. `Free accessibility report for [domain]`
2. `[domain] WCAG audit - no charge`
3. `Your free accessibility score: [X]/100`
4. `Complimentary site scan for [Company]`
5. `[domain] accessibility check (free)`

---

## FEAR-BASED (Use Carefully)

1. `Fashion Nova paid $5M - [domain] has similar issues`
2. `[domain]: lawsuit exposure detected`
3. `Before you get a demand letter...`
4. `[X] accessibility violations = legal risk`
5. `ADA lawsuit risk: [domain]`

---

## PERSONALIZED

1. `[Name], [domain] accessibility needs work`
2. `For [Name] at [Company] - site scan results`
3. `[Name] - quick accessibility question`
4. `Thought [Company] should know about this`
5. `[Name], saw your site at [Company]...`

## WINNER PREDICTIONS (Based on B2B email data)

**Likely Best Performers:**
1. `[domain]: [X] critical accessibility issues found` (specific + urgent)
2. `Quick question about [domain]'s accessibility` (curiosity + non-threatening)
3. `Free accessibility report for [domain]` (value-first)

**Test Strategy:**
- Send Version A to first 5 leads
- Send Version B to next 5 leads
- Send Version C to next 5 leads
- Double down on best performer
