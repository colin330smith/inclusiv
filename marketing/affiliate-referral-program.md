## Tier 1: Affiliate Program (15% First Payment)

### Who It's For
- Bloggers and content creators
- Social media influencers
- Newsletter operators
- Community managers
- Anyone with an audience interested in accessibility/e-commerce

### How It Works
1. Sign up at inclusiv.eu/affiliates (self-serve)
2. Get unique tracking link
3. Share with audience
4. Earn 15% of first payment for each signup
5. Monthly payouts via Stripe Connect

### Commission Structure
| Plan | Monthly Price | Your Commission |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| Starter | €49 | €7.35 |
| Growth | €149 | €22.35 |
| Agency | €399 | €59.85 |

### Requirements
- None - anyone can join
- No minimum payouts
- No exclusivity

### Tools Provided
- Unique referral link
- Banner images (multiple sizes)
- Pre-written social posts
- Email swipe copy
- Real-time analytics dashboard

### How It Works

2. Brief qualification call (15 min)
3. Get partner dashboard access
4. Refer clients who need accessibility scanning
5. Earn 20% of ALL revenue they generate (lifetime)

### Commission Structure

## Tier 3: Reseller Partner (40% Wholesale Discount)

### Who It's For
- Digital agencies (10+ clients)
- E-commerce development shops
- Marketing agencies
- Platform consultants
- Regional IT providers

### How It Works
1. Apply at inclusiv.eu/partners/reseller
2. Qualification call + agreement signing
3. Get wholesale pricing access
4. Buy at 40% off, resell at your price
5. You own billing, support, client relationship

## Tier 4: White-Label Partner (Custom Pricing)

### Who It's For
- Large agencies (50+ clients)
- E-commerce platforms
- SaaS companies
- Hosting providers
- Platform ecosystem partners

### How It Works
1. Contact partnerships@inclusiv.eu
2. Discovery call + technical assessment
3. Custom agreement negotiation
4. Full platform deployment
5. You operate under your brand

### What's Included
- **Full brand removal** - 100% your branding
- **Custom domain** - scanner.youragency.com
- **API access** - full read/write capabilities
- **Custom integrations** - connect to your tools
- **Dedicated support** - direct Slack channel
- **SLA guarantee** - 99.9% uptime
- **Training** - for your team
- **Co-marketing** - joint case studies, PR

### Pricing Model
| Component | Pricing |
|-----------|---------|
| Platform License | €2,997/year (or €297/month) |
| Per-Scan Pricing | €0.50/page (volume discounts) |
| Support Add-On | €997/year (optional SLA upgrade) |
| Custom Development | €150/hour (API customization) |

### Volume Discounts
| Monthly Scans | Per-Page Price |
|---------------|----------------|
| 0-10,000 | €0.50 |
| 10,001-50,000 | €0.35 |
| 50,001-100,000 | €0.20 |
| 100,000+ | Custom |

### Example ROI
**Agency with 100 e-commerce clients:**
- Platform cost: €297/mo
- 100 clients × 50 pages avg = 5,000 pages/mo × €0.50 = €2,500/mo
- Total cost: €2,797/mo
- Charge clients €49/mo each = €4,900/mo revenue
- **Monthly profit: €2,103**

### Requirements
- 50+ potential end clients
- Technical team for integration
- Committed go-to-market plan
- Annual agreement

## Payment & Payout Details

### Affiliate Tier
- **Payout method:** Stripe Connect (automated)
- **Payout schedule:** Monthly (5th of each month)
- **Minimum payout:** €10
- **Cookie duration:** 90 days

### Referral Tier
- **Payout method:** Stripe Connect or Invoice
- **Payout schedule:** Monthly (5th of each month)
- **Minimum payout:** €50
- **Cookie duration:** 90 days
- **Attribution:** Last-click + partner dashboard tracking

### Reseller Tier
- **Billing:** Monthly invoice or annual prepay (5% discount)
- **Payment terms:** Net 30
- **Credit line:** Available after 3 months good standing

### White-Label Tier
- **Billing:** Annual agreement, monthly usage billing
- **Payment terms:** Net 30
- **Early termination:** Pro-rated refund minus 3 months

## How to Join

### Affiliate
1. Go to inclusiv.eu/affiliates
2. Enter email, create account
3. Get your link immediately
4. Start sharing

### Referral Partner
1. Go to inclusiv.eu/partners/referral
2. Fill out application (5 min)
3. Schedule 15-min qualification call
4. Get approved within 24 hours

### Reseller Partner
1. Go to inclusiv.eu/partners/reseller
2. Fill out application with client details
3. Schedule 30-min discovery call
4. Sign reseller agreement
5. Get onboarded within 1 week

### White-Label Partner
1. Email partnerships@inclusiv.eu
2. Initial discovery call (30 min)
3. Technical assessment (1 week)
4. Custom proposal delivery
5. Negotiation and signing
6. Implementation (2-4 weeks)
