# Inclusiv Competitive Intelligence Report
## Strategic Competitive Analysis & Market Positioning

*Last Updated: January 2026*

## Executive Summary

The web accessibility market is experiencing explosive growth driven by:
- **European Accessibility Act (EAA)** enforcement beginning June 28, 2025
- **ADA lawsuits** projected at 5,000+ in 2025 (20% increase YoY)
- **101 million EU citizens** (1 in 4 over age 16) affected by accessibility barriers
- **FTC crackdown** on overlay providers ($1M fine against accessiBe in April 2025)

**Inclusiv's opportunity**: Position as the **EAA-first, code-level solution** that actually fixes accessibility issues rather than masking them with overlays.

### Inclusiv's Four Pillars of Differentiation

#### 1. EAA-Specific Focus (vs Generic Accessibility)

**The Problem**: Most tools focus on ADA/WCAG generically. The EAA has specific requirements:
- EN 301 549 technical standard
- Mandatory accessibility statements
- E-commerce service requirements
- June 2025 enforcement deadline

**Inclusiv's Advantage**:
- Built specifically for EAA compliance
- EN 301 549 mapping
- Accessibility statement generator
- E-commerce specific checks
- EU market focus

**Messaging**: *"The only accessibility platform built specifically for European Accessibility Act compliance."*

#### 2. Instant Scanning (vs Consulting/Overlays)

**The Problem**:
- Consulting: Weeks/months for audits, $10K-$100K costs
- Overlays: Instant but don't actually work (lawsuits prove this)

**Inclusiv's Advantage**:
- Scan results in seconds
- No waiting for consultants
- No fake overlay "fixes"
- Real issues identified immediately
- Continuous monitoring

**Messaging**: *"Know your EAA compliance status in 60 seconds, not 60 days."*

#### 3. AI-Powered Code Fixes (vs Just Problem Lists)

**The Problem**: Every tool tells you what's wrong. None tell you how to fix it.
- WAVE: "Missing alt text" - okay, now what?
- Lighthouse: Score of 72 - but what code changes?
- Siteimprove: 847 issues found - developer overwhelmed

**Inclusiv's Advantage**:
- Identifies the problem
- Shows the exact code location
- **Generates the fix code**
- Copy-paste remediation
- AI-powered fix suggestions

**Messaging**: *"Don't just find problems. Fix them. AI-generated code fixes you can implement in minutes."*

#### 4. E-Commerce Focus (vs General Websites)

**The Problem**: E-commerce has unique accessibility challenges:
- Product image alt text at scale
- Checkout flow accessibility
- Cart and filter interactions
- Price and availability announcements
- Form validation for purchases

**Inclusiv's Advantage**:
- Shopify app integration
- WooCommerce plugin
- E-commerce specific rules
- Product-focused scanning
- Checkout flow analysis

**Messaging**: *"Built for online stores. Because your checkout matters more than your blog."*
