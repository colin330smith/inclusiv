## Partner Type Definitions

### Referral Partner (20% Revenue Share)
- Send clients to Inclusiv
- Earn commission on all client revenue
- No technical integration required
- Best for: Consultancies, smaller agencies

### Reseller Partner (40% Wholesale Discount)
- Resell Inclusiv at your margin
- Own the billing relationship
- White-label reports available
- Best for: Mid-size agencies with 10+ clients

### White-Label Partner (Custom Pricing)
- Full brand removal
- Custom domain (scanner.youragency.com)
- API access for integration
- Best for: Large agencies, platform partners

### Technology Integration Partner
- API/SDK integration
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Mutual referrals
- Best for: Complementary accessibility tools
