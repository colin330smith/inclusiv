## Mission Statement

To make digital accessibility achievable for every e-commerce business, ensuring online shopping is available to everyone regardless of ability.

---

## The Problem We Solve

The European Accessibility Act (EAA) requires all e-commerce services to meet WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards by June 28, 2025.

**The challenge:**
- 1.2 million+ EU e-commerce sites must comply
- 97% currently fail accessibility standards
- Traditional compliance costs €10,000-€100,000+
- Most businesses don't know where to start
- Non-compliance penalties up to €100,000 per violation

---

## Our Solution

Inclusiv is an AI-powered accessibility compliance platform that:

1. **Scans** — Automated analysis identifies accessibility issues across entire websites
2. **Prioritises** — Issues ranked by legal risk and user impact
3. **Guides** — Plain-language fix instructions anyone can follow
4. **Monitors** — Continuous scanning as content changes
5. **Documents** — Automated compliance evidence and accessibility statements

## Key Statistics

### The Regulatory Landscape
- **June 28, 2025:** EAA enforcement begins
- **Directive 2019/882:** The legal basis
- **WCAG 2.1 AA:** The technical standard
- **27 EU member states:** Implementing national laws

### The Accessibility Gap
- **97%** of e-commerce sites fail WCAG standards
- **83%** have critical issues blocking disabled users
- **47** average violations per page
- **89%** missing alt text on images
- **76%** poor colour contrast

### The Business Case
- **20%** conversion rate increase from accessibility improvements
- **15%** of global population has a disability
- **€2 trillion** annual spending power of disabled consumers globally
- **SEO benefits:** Accessibility and search optimisation overlap significantly
