## Pitch Strategy by Tier

### Tier 1 (Major Tech)
- Lead with regulatory angle and market size
- Emphasize AI/automation differentiation
- Offer exclusive data or early access
- Best timing: Tuesday-Thursday morning

### Tier 2 (E-commerce Trade)
- Lead with practical business impact
- Emphasize ROI and conversion benefits
- Offer case studies and how-to content
- Best timing: Monday-Wednesday

### Tier 3 (EU Business)
- Lead with regulatory compliance angle
- Emphasize legal risks and deadlines
- Offer expert commentary on EAA
- Best timing: Around policy announcements

### Tier 4 (Accessibility)
- Lead with user impact and inclusion
- Emphasize genuine accessibility benefits
- Avoid "compliance-only" messaging
- Best timing: Disability awareness dates

## Follow-Up Protocol

1. **Initial pitch**: Personalized email
2. **Follow-up 1**: 3-4 days later, brief check-in
3. **Follow-up 2**: 1 week later, new angle or data
4. **Social engagement**: Like/retweet their content between emails
5. **Move on**: After 3 attempts, add to nurture list

---

## Notes

- Always personalize pitches with recent article references
- Respect journalist preferences (check Twitter bios)
- Build relationships before big asks
- Offer exclusive angles to top-tier targets
- Track all outreach in CRM
