## QUICK REFERENCE

### Best Posting Times
- **Reddit**: 9-11am EST (Tue-Thu)
- **HN**: 8am EST (Tue-Wed)
- **LinkedIn**: 8am EST or 8am CET
- **Twitter**: 9am, 12pm, 5pm EST

### Response Time Targets
- Reddit comments: < 2 hours (critical for algorithm)
- LinkedIn comments: < 4 hours
- Email inquiries: < 24 hours
- DMs: < 12 hours

### Daily Minimums (Non-Negotiable)
- 1 piece of content posted
- 20 outreach messages sent
- 10 engagement comments made
- 10 new connections requested
- All comments responded to

---

## EMERGENCY PLAYBOOK

### If scans are low:
1. Post in a new subreddit
2. Find accessibility discussion threads and comment with scanner link
3. Share a "scan your competitor" post

### If no email captures:
1. Check email gate is working
2. Make value prop clearer on scan results
3. Try exit-intent popup

### If no trial signups:
1. Personally email everyone who scanned in last 7 days
2. Offer extended trial
3. Ask what's holding them back

### If no paid conversions:
1. Call trial users (yes, call them)
2. Offer 50% off first month
3. Offer concierge onboarding
