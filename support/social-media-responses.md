# Inclusiv Social Media Response Templates

## Overview

This document provides pre-written responses for common social media interactions. Customize as needed, but maintain a friendly, helpful, and professional tone.

---

## Platform Guidelines

### Twitter/X
- Keep responses under 280 characters when possible
- Use thread for longer explanations
- Always tag @tryinclusiv in responses
- Use relevant hashtags sparingly (#a11y, #accessibility)

### LinkedIn
- More professional tone
- Longer responses acceptable
- Share helpful resources
- Engage with industry content

### Product Hunt / G2 / Capterra
- Thank all reviewers
- Address specific feedback
- Highlight new features
- Never argue with negative reviews

## Quick Response Guidelines

### DO:
- Respond within 4 hours to direct mentions
- Thank people for positive feedback
- Acknowledge issues publicly, resolve privately
- Be helpful, even to non-customers
- Tag relevant team members for technical questions
- Follow up on unresolved issues

### DON'T:
- Ignore negative comments
- Argue publicly
- Make promises you can't keep
- Share customer information publicly
- Disparage competitors
- Use excessive marketing speak

### ESCALATE TO MANAGEMENT:
- Threats of legal action
- Major service complaints going viral
- Press/media inquiries
- Influential accounts with complaints
- Security or privacy concerns
